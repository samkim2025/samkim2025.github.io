Face Morphing

Overview

In this project, I tried to create a morph between two portraits -- mine and Bill's.

I also computed the mean face of a population dataset, and generate caricatures by extrapolating from the population mean.

I achieved this through image warping, cross-dissolving colors, and manipulating facial geometries using keypoints and Delauney triangulation.

Part 1

First, I manually selected keypoints on my image and another image to mark corresponding facial features.

I then computed a Delaunay triangulation based on the average positions of these keypoints to facilitate accurate mapping between the two faces.
